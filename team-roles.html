Who Does What?

Technical Roles

Mechanical Lead

Prototypes designs with cardboard, then builds the final robot using CAD and metal parts.

Electrical Specialist

Manages "wire spaghetti". Ensures cables are tied down, sensors are plugged in correctly, and batteries are charged.

Software Engineer

Writes the TeleOp (driver) and Autonomous code. Works closely with Hardware to test mechanisms.

Business Roles

Team Captain

The project manager. Sets deadlines (e.g., "Drivebase done by Nov 1"). Resolves arguments.

Outreach & Marketing

Runs Instagram, designs the pit banner, and connects with local pros for mentorship.

Documentation Lead

Crucial Role. Compiles the Engineering Portfolio. You cannot win top awards without this.

Remember: Everyone Scouts!

At competitions, every team member who isn't driving or fixing the robot should be "Scouting" (watching other matches) to find good alliance partners.
