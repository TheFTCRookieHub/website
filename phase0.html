Subject: Future Engineers at [High School Name] need your help!

Dear [Company Name],

We are Team [Number], a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team from [City]. We are students learning to design, build, and code robots from scratch.

We admire [Company]'s work in [Industry] and would love to put your logo on our robot. We are looking for sponsors to help us cover our registration fees ($295) and parts costs.

The Team Contract

Prevent drama before it starts. Have every student sign this.

  • Attendance: "I commit to attending 80% of meetings."
  • Communication: "I will check Discord/Slack daily."
  • Safety: "I will wear safety glasses whenever a robot is on."
  • Gracious Professionalism: "I will respect my teammates' ideas."

3. The Workspace

You don't need a high-tech lab. A garage, classroom, or basement works fine. Essentials:

  • Storage: Bins for parts. If you lose parts, you lose money.
  • Power: Dedicated outlets for charging batteries.
  • Internet: Reliable WiFi for research and coding.
  • Field Data: You need roughly 12x12ft for a full field, but 8x8ft works for half-field practice.
