Master the Manuals

Game Manual Part 1 (The Tournament)

Think of this as the "Law of the Land". It covers robot rules, advancement, and conduct.

CRITICAL SECTIONS:

  • Robot Inspection: Your robot MUST fit in an 18"x18"x18" box basically.
  • General Robot Rules: No sharp edges, no hazardous materials.
  • System Rules: Rules about specific motors (GoBilda, REV, Tetrix) and batteries.

Game Manual Part 2 (The Game)

This changes every year. It describes the field, scoring, and penalties.

WHAT TO LOOK FOR:

  • Scoring Breakdown: Exactly how many points is a "Park" vs a "Pixel"?
  • Penalties: Avoid "Major Penalties" (usually 30 points!) at all costs.
  • Endgame: The last 30 seconds usually offer high points for unique tasks (drones, hanging).

The Golden Rule

The Official Q&A Forum is the ONLY source of official answers besides the manual. Discord and Reddit are often wrong.
