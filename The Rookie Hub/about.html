Our Mission

The FTC Rookie Hub was born from a simple observation: starting in FTC can be overwhelming. With thousands of pages of manuals, complex coding environments, and advanced engineering concepts, new teams often feel lost.

The "Rookie-First" Approach

We believe that the future of STEM depends on making the first step as easy as possible. This site is designed to be a "safe harbor" where there are no "stupid" questions and where mentorship is the priority.

"Chief Delphi, but made for rookies."

Who Built This?

This website is built by an FTC team who remembers exactly what it was like to be a rookie. We've been through the long nights, the magic-smoke-releasing shorts, and the joy of finally seeing the robot move.
